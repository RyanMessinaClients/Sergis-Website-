01Our story

Three decades of glass, installed by experts.

Sergi's Images Inc. was founded in 1991 by Louis Sergi, who began glazing at sixteen. He spent the years that followed learning every aspect of the trade — and then opened a shop in Red Hook.

The shop is still here. It has since acquired the adjoining 14,000 sq ft warehouse. We specialize in commercial glazing at every scale and residential work of the most exacting kind.

What hasn't changed in thirty years: the desire to leave each project completed with the client's utmost satisfaction, ensuring them as a returning customer.
